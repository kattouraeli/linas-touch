About Lina’s Touch

Mastering Nail Beauty for 25 Years

With over 25 years of experience, Lina’s Touch is more than a nail bar, it’s a commitment to precision and care. Lina’s expertise goes beyond aesthetics; she ensures every client receives meticulous attention, whether it’s a perfect manicure or a pedicure that prioritizes foot health. Her dedication means she never stops until every detail is flawless, creating an experience that’s as thorough as it is relaxing.

Lina’s Touch, beauty isn’t just about polish—it’s about confidence, comfort, and trust. Every session is personalized to meet your needs, whether it’s restoring nail health, perfecting intricate details, or simply unwinding in a private, welcoming setting. Lina’s unwavering attention to her craft ensures that every client leaves feeling refreshed, pampered, and taken care of.
